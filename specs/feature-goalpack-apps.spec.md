# Feature: Goal Pack — Applications & Paperwork

## Intent
Reduce admin thrash; ensure completion of key documents for targeted programs.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. “Program card” template with fields: Deadlines, SOP, CV, References, Transcripts.
2. Split queues: **Deep** (SOP draft, research synthesis) vs **Shallow** (emails, uploads).
3. Deadline alerts appear in Weekly Review; important-not-urgent items scheduled.
4. Export a per-program status snapshot.

## Notes
- Keep shallow work out of deep slots; enforce WIP in Doing.
